Effects of Corruption

Impact on Society and Development

How Corruption Affects Us

Economic Impact

Reduced economic growth, increased poverty, and widening income inequality.

Social Impact

Deterioration of public services, reduced access to education and healthcare.

Political Impact

Weakened democratic institutions, reduced public trust in government.

Environmental Impact

Environmental degradation due to bypassing regulations and proper oversight.
