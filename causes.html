Understanding Why Corruption Occurs

Major Causes of Corruption

Weak Institutions

Poor governance structures and weak institutional frameworks that fail to prevent corrupt practices.

Lack of Transparency

Absence of clear procedures and public oversight in decision-making processes.

Economic Factors

Low wages, poverty, and economic inequality creating incentives for corrupt behavior.

Cultural Factors

Social norms and practices that may inadvertently encourage or tolerate corruption.
