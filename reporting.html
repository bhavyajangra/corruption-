How to Report Corruption

Government Hotlines

Contact dedicated anti-corruption hotlines to report cases of corruption in government institutions.

Online Platforms

Use secure online platforms and websites designed for anonymous reporting of corruption.

Legal Authorities

Report to law enforcement agencies, anti-corruption bureaus, or relevant legal authorities.

NGOs and Watchdogs

Connect with anti-corruption organizations and civil society groups that handle corruption reports.
