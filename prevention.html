Prevention Methods

Stopping Corruption Before It Starts

Key Prevention Strategies

Strong Institutions

Building robust governance systems with checks and balances to prevent corrupt practices.

Public Awareness

Educating citizens about their rights and the importance of integrity in public service.

Whistleblower Protection

Implementing strong protections for those who report corruption and misconduct.

Digital Systems

Using technology to increase transparency and reduce opportunities for corruption.
