Types of Corruption

Understanding Different Forms of Corruption

Common Forms of Corruption

Bribery

The offering, giving, receiving, or soliciting of something of value for the purpose of influencing the action of an official.

Embezzlement

The misappropriation of funds or resources entrusted to one's care for personal gain.

Fraud

Deliberate deception to secure unfair or unlawful gain, including document forgery and identity theft.

Nepotism

Favoritism shown to relatives or close friends by those in power, especially in professional appointments.
